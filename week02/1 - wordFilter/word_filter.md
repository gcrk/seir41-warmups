# Filter

Your task is to remove all duplicate words from a string, leaving only single (first) words entries.

## Example:

Input:

'alpha beta beta gamma gamma gamma delta alpha beta beta gamma gamma gamma delta'

Output:

'alpha beta gamma delta'
